Services

Basic Informational Websites

A basic website, custom designed for your business or organization. Informational websites contain information that does not change frequently. These websites often contain:

  • Explanations of what your business/organization does
  • Hours of operation
  • Address and directions
  • Contact information and/or a contact form
  • Simple "product" information
    • Restaurant menus
    • Programming offerings or available services
    • Brands carried
  • Staff or faculty listing

Dynamic Websites

If your organization has a need for a website with fast-moving content, we can help. Whether your website needs a few dynamic pages, or you require a full content management system, we can provide an easy to use way to keep your own website up to date. Features of dynamic websites include:

  • Organizational or specialized blogs
  • Photo galleries with user submissions
  • Product catalogs that change frequently
  • Shopping Carts
  • User comments
  • Pages can be changed, added or removed easily

Custom Development

We have experience developing in several languages and frameworks. If you require a custom plugin for your CMS, web integration with legacy in-house software, or features added to an open source project that your organization uses, we can help.
